# Doctoral Meta-Analyses: Integrative Ethnomedicine & Interdisciplinary Health Sciences

A curated collection of **19 doctoral-level meta-analyses and comprehensive integrative literature syntheses** exploring the intersections of traditional knowledge, cultural practices, and cutting-edge scientific research.

### Scope & Topics

This repository synthesizes research across:

- **Ethnobotany, Ethnopharmacology & Pharmacognosy**
- **Ethnomedicine, Ethnobiology & Ethnomicrobiology**
- **Medical Anthropology & Cultural Ethnography of Healing**
- **Integrative Medicine & Traditional Healing Systems**
- **Botany, Forestry (ethnobotany-focused), and Ethnozoology**
- **Bioengineering, Drug Discovery & Modern Pharmacology**
- **Bioethics in Global Health and Traditional Knowledge**
- **Quantum Biology** and its implications for ethnomedicine and integrative health

### Purpose

These works function as **interdisciplinary bridges** between:
- Indigenous and traditional knowledge systems
- Contemporary biomedical science
- Emerging fields such as quantum biology, systems biology, and integrative health
- Ethical, cultural, and policy dimensions of bioprospecting and medical innovation

Each meta-analysis employs rigorous **integrative review** and **synthesis methodologies**, often reading as "Comprehensive Integrative Reviews" that identify patterns, knowledge gaps, translational opportunities, and future research directions across vast bodies of literature.

### Key Themes
- Preservation and scientific validation of traditional medicinal knowledge
- Bioprospecting and sustainable development of plant- and nature-derived therapeutics
- Cultural contexts of health, illness, and healing
- Ethical frameworks for collaborating with indigenous communities
- Convergence of ancient ethnomedical wisdom with frontier sciences (including quantum biology)
- Translational pathways from ethnobotany → drug discovery → clinical application

### Repository Contents
- Full meta-analysis documents (PDFs)
- Executive summaries and key findings
- Literature matrices and synthesis frameworks
- Supporting datasets and bibliographies
- Related simulations and conceptual frameworks (quantum biology, virtual quantum DNA, etc.)
